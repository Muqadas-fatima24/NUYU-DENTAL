Transform Your Smile
with Invisalign
Invisalign is a revolutionary orthodontic treatment that uses a series of clear,
custom made aligners to gradually shift your teeth into the desired position.
Whether you want to correct crooked teeth, overbites, or gaps, Invisalign
offers a discreet and comfortable way to achieve a beautiful smile.
Say goodbye to metal brackets and wires. Invisalign aligners are virtually
invisible and can be removed whenever you need, making it easier to
maintain your oral hygiene and enjoy your favorite foods.
Aftercare and Support
At Regent Dental, our team is dedicated to supporting you throughout your Invisalign® treatment. We
ensure youre comfortable and confident every step of the way, and help maintain your new smile long
after the treatment is complete. Invisalign® aligners provide a discreet and effective path to straighter
teeth, improving both your dental health and self-esteem with minimal impact on your day-to-day life.
Discrete Benefits
Subtle and Comfortable
Invisalign® aligners are made from patented, medical-grade translucent plastic, making them lightweight and discreet. The custom fit blends seamlessly with your teeth, letting your natural smile shine. Its smooth, comfortable design exerts gentle forces that are barely noticeable, even while you sleep.
